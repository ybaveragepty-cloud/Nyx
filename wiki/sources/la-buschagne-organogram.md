# La Buschagne Organogram

**Source:** ChatGPT conversation (~2,564 lines)
**Date:** ~2026-03-03
**GPT:** La Buschagne Aquaponic Farming Empire

## Summary

Defines the full legal/corporate structure for Richard's growing empire. Moves from single (Pty) Ltd to a multi-entity holding group under a family trust. Establishes YBA as the unified institutional brand name distinct from the heritage farm brand.

## Final Corporate Structure

```
Family Trust
    ↓
YBA Group Holdings (Pty) Ltd
    ↓
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    │                │         │
YBA Agricultural   YBA Aquaponics   YBA       YBA Property
Systems            (Operating       Infra     Holdings
(IP & Licensing)   Farm)           (Build &   (Land & Assets)
                                   Engineering)
                   + JV Companies (each as separate SPV)
```

## Key Decisions

- **Family Trust** at the top — for generational wealth and estate protection
- **No external shareholders** in Holdings or IP company — family control only
- **Property Holdings** separates land from operational risk (if farm fails, land survives)
- **JV companies** as separate SPVs for each partnership/joint venture
- **YBA = "Why Be Average"** — 10-year personal brand philosophy, now institutional group name
- **First hire needed:** Operations Manager (Richard currently holds all roles)

## Entity Purposes

| Entity | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| YBA Group Holdings | Control center, owns all subsidiaries |
| YBA Agricultural Systems | IP, licensing, franchise fees |
| YBA Aquaponics / Labuschagne Aquaponics | Day-to-day farming operations |
| YBA Infrastructure | Farm builds, engineering projects |
| YBA Property Holdings | Land and physical assets |
| JV SPVs | Each partnership isolated in its own company |

## Strategic Rationale

- Isolates risk between entities
- Enables scalability and investor entry at subsidiary level
- Builds institutional credibility with banks/investors
- Clean generational transfer through trust structure
- Heritage brand (La Buschagne/Labuschagne) for farm operations, YBA for institutional/corporate
