## Key Technical Knowledge

- **Ammonia testing:** Daily minimum in commercial systems (not weekly like hobby)
- **Nitrification sequence:** Ammonia spikes first if biofilter fails (AOB → NOB)
- **DWC as emergency buffer:** Acts as sump, leak buffer, pump delay buffer
- **Trout DO:** Never below 6 mg/L, target 8+ mg/L
- **Tilapia DO:** Survive 4-5 mg/L, thrive 6-7 mg/L

## Design Philosophy

> Operate at 80% of system capability. Operators who push 100% burn out or crash.

Build for **maximum lifetime profit per system**, not maximum profit per cycle.
