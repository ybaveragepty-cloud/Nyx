## Summary

Formalizes the YBA brand architecture, establishes the "Branded House" model, defines sub-brand colors and visual identity, and reveals the full product portfolio beyond farming.

## Key Revelation

> **YBA = "Why Be Average"**

## Brand Architecture: Branded House

All products under the YBA master brand, not separate brands. [[ybaflow]] is the platform layer; all other products are built on top of it.

**Green** is the shared master-brand anchor across all.

## Terra AI

A named sub-component of [[ybaterra]] — an AI decision-making/optimization layer that:
- Monitors environmental data
- Adjusts conditions automatically
- Optimizes yield
- Learns over time

Distinct from YBATerra itself; Terra AI is the intelligence inside the farm platform.

## Visual Identity

- Oval badge logo system
- Metallic + glow aesthetic
- Green core with modular sub-brand color differentiation
- Logos generated for each sub-brand
