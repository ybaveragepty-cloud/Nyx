# Branch · Brand Architecture Strategy

**Source:** ChatGPT conversation (~1,100 lines)
**Date:** ~2026-04-13
**Origin:** https://chatgpt.com/g/g-p-69b271c633e08191bcbd104077ab77d7-ybaflow/

## Summary

A continuation and refinement of [[brand-architecture-strategy]]. Clarifies the role hierarchy between YBA brands and makes one significant color/positioning change.

## Key Refinement: YBA Flow = Public-Facing Entry Point

YBA Flow is repositioned as the **ecosystem's primary customer interface** (like Microsoft.com or Apple.com), not just another product:

- **YBA Flow** = customer entry point, public-facing platform layer
- **YBA Systems** = hidden infrastructure/SaaS backend (like Azure)
- **Products (Terra, Med, etc.)** = branded implementations customers use

## Color Change

- **YBA Flow changed from blue → green** to align with core YBA identity, since it now carries the master brand forward as the public face
- **YBA Agency assigned purple** (for strategic distinction)
- **YBA Systems remains silver** (hidden infrastructure)

## Brand Behavior Rule

Same color doesn't mean same visual treatment — Flow should feel more dynamic/alive than core YBA, even though both use green.
