**Source:** ChatGPT project conversation (~816 lines)

## Summary

Early strategic thinking about how Richard should organize his ChatGPT usage and explore AI agents for farm operations. Predates the YBAFlow tech build entirely. Reveals personal context: Richard is studying for an electrician trade test, managing marriage stress, training/fasting, and running the farm simultaneously.

## Thread Organization System

Decided to use focused, topic-specific ChatGPT threads:
1. **Operations** — fish health, water params, growth, irrigation, stocking
2. **Strategy & Expansion** — new farms, legal, partnerships, capital, market
3. **Personal Performance** — fasting, gym, sleep, marriage, trade test, discipline
4. **Weekly Review** (optional) — wins, failures, revenue, priorities

## AI Agent Concept (Pre-OpenClaw)

Four agent "roles" proposed:
1. **Operations Manager** — fish logs, water params, anomaly detection
2. **Expansion & Strategy Officer** — partnerships, CAPEX, legal, investment
3. **Knowledge Librarian** — converts notes to SOPs, indexes summaries
4. **Personal Performance Director** — tracks health, discipline, trade test progress

> "Agents only work if you consistently feed them structured input. If you're chaotic, the agents become chaotic."

## Tool Recommendations

- ChatGPT = strategic brain
- Notion or Obsidian = operational command center
- Google Sheets = financial/production tracking
- Home Assistant = water temp, power failure alerts, pump monitoring
- Inkbird/Sonoff = cheap automation relays
