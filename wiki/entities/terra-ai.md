# Terra AI

An AI decision-making and optimization layer **within** [[ybaterra]]. Not a standalone product — it's the intelligence inside the farm platform.

## Capabilities

- Monitors environmental data
- Adjusts conditions automatically
- Optimizes yield
- Learns over time

## Positioning

Terra AI is the "AI farming assistant" — the part of YBATerra that makes it intelligent rather than just a data entry system.
