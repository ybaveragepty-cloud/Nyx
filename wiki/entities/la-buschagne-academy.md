# La Buschagne Academy

The planned training and certification arm of the La Buschagne Group. Will train franchise managers and certify operators to run aquaponic farms to Richard's engineering standards.

## Role in Franchise Model

- Trains managers before they operate a franchise farm
- Certifies competency levels
- Provides the course/blueprint that filters serious operators from hobbyists
- Only top performers qualify for franchise partnership

## Course Structure (Draft)

6 modules: Foundations → Fish System Design → Plant Production → System Calculations → Economics → Scaling Up
