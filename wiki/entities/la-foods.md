# LA Foods

Richard's food/packhouse business. The first real-world user of the [[packhouse-admin-copilot]].

## Operations

- Packhouse operations (packing, distribution)
- Farm harvesting
- Market buying
- Customer orders (restaurants, retail)
- Supplier management

## Communication Channels

- **Gmail:** orders@lafoods.co.za
- **WhatsApp Business:** single mixed-use inbox (personal, retail customers, restaurant orders, supplier updates)

## Key Constraint

The single WhatsApp inbox mixes personal, order, and supplier messages — requires an AI triage layer with confidence scoring.
