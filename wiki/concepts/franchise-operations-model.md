# Franchise Operations Model

The La Buschagne franchise model: train managers, franchise full farms, control product distribution through the GMP packhouse. This is not a consulting model or a licensing model — it is full-farm franchising with centralized quality control and exclusive product routing.

## Core Structure

The franchise model has three pillars:

1. **Train managers** — [[la-buschagne-academy]] certifies operators to La Buschagne standards before they touch a farm
2. **Franchise full farms** — Partners operate complete production facilities under the La Buschagne brand, not just components
3. **Exclusive distribution** — All product flows through the La Buschagne GMP packhouse. No independent selling. This is the revenue control mechanism and the quality enforcement point

> Primary goal: Train managers, franchise full farms (not consult or license only)

## Entry Tiers

Two paths into the network:

- **Greenfield:** New farm built to spec from day one. 100% compliance from the start. This is the preferred model
- **Legacy farms:** Existing operations that want to join the network. They enter as Provisional Network Members with a 12-18 month upgrade path to full compliance. Any farm that cannot meet standards within the window is not admitted

## Deviation Policy

Any change to biomass levels, species, or system configuration requires written HQ approval. This is not bureaucracy — it is the mechanism that prevents franchise partners from cutting corners that compromise the [[200-percent-engineering-doctrine]].

## Network Culture

> "Performance-driven brotherhood" — earned membership, KPI-based.

The La Buschagne network is explicitly not a loose association. Membership is earned through demonstrated operational excellence. KPIs are tracked, performance is reviewed, and the brand identity of "engineered for safety, operated for precision" is maintained through active enforcement.

## The 12-Month Institutionalization Roadmap

The franchise system itself is being built on a structured timeline: months 1-3 are documentation sprint (2-3 hrs/day with ChatGPT), months 3-6 are GMP certification, months 6-9 are operational proof, and months 9-12 are investor/capital activation. The roadmap turns the franchise concept into a deployable system.
