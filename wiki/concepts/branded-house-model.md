# Branded House Model

The brand architecture where the parent brand (YBA / "Why Be Average") is the primary identity, and all sub-brands are descriptive extensions rather than independent identities. Every product lives under the YBA umbrella. Customers know they are buying a YBA product; the sub-brand tells them which vertical.

## Brand Hierarchy

| Layer | Brand | Role | Color |
|-------|-------|------|-------|
| Master | YBA | Parent identity | Green |
| Platform | [[ybaflow]] | Public-facing entry point, ecosystem interface | Green (dynamic) |
| Infrastructure | [[yba-systems]] | Hidden backend, SaaS infrastructure | Silver |
| Product | [[ybaterra]] | Agriculture/farming vertical | Gold/amber |
| Product | [[yba-agency]] | Strategy, consulting, execution | Purple |
| Product | [[yba-med]] | Health systems | Orange/amber |
| Product | [[yba-realty]] | Property, land, assets | Blue |

Green is the shared master-brand anchor. Sub-brand colors differentiate verticals without breaking the family identity.

## Flow vs. Systems: The Two Faces

A key refinement from [[branch-brand-architecture]]: YBA Flow is the public-facing customer interface (like Microsoft.com or Apple.com), while [[yba-systems]] is the hidden infrastructure layer (like Azure). Customers interact with Flow; Systems runs silently underneath.

> Same color doesn't mean same visual treatment — Flow should feel more dynamic/alive than core YBA, even though both use green.

This separation matters because it allows the infrastructure brand to remain invisible to customers while maintaining its own identity for internal and B2B contexts.

## Why Branded House (Not House of Brands)

The alternative — a house of brands — would give each product its own independent identity (think Procter & Gamble, where Tide and Pampers have no visible connection). Richard rejected this because:

1. **YBA is small** — brand equity needs to compound in one place, not fragment across independent names
2. **Cross-selling** — a customer who trusts YBA Terra should naturally trust YBA Med
3. **Operational efficiency** — one visual system, one design language, one brand story

## La Buschagne Brand (Farm Side)

The La Buschagne brand operates as a separate branded house for the aquaponics franchise empire — La Buschagne Fresh/Select (produce), La Buschagne Blue (aquaculture), La Buschagne Crafted/Artisan (value-add), and [[la-buschagne-academy]] (training). This is the farming identity, distinct from the tech identity of YBA.
