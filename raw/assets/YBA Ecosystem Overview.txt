What YBA is becoming

YBA is being structured as a layered ecosystem, not a single business. The intended hierarchy is: YBA Systems as the parent/infrastructure layer, YBA Flow as the SaaS/automation commercial layer, and YBA Terra as the first vertical product built on top of Flow. The same pattern is intended for later verticals such as real estate, medical, and agency products. 

 


What each brand should do

YBA Systems
This is the infrastructure and ownership layer. It is meant to host internal services, APIs, automation engines, monitoring, and the technical backbone customers do not directly see. It is also the legal/IP/control layer. 


YBA Flow
This is the customer-facing automation business and SaaS platform. It is the commercial face you market. It should explain solutions, capture leads, and become the platform on which multiple vertical products run. It is intentionally not supposed to be locked into agriculture only. 

 


YBA Terra
This is the first serious vertical product. It is intended to be the agriculture/farm operations system powered by Flow. The defined direction is for Terra to eventually cover operations dashboards, sensors, inventory, order management, staff management, automation rules, and an AI assistant layer.
