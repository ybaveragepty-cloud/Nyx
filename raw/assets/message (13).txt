Grants for primary production, infrastructure, irrigation, and emerging farmers.

Pros

Non-repayable

Infrastructure support

Sometimes equipment subsidies

Can unlock credibility for future funding

Cons

Slow processing (6–18 months)

Often politically influenced

Paperwork heavy

Usually targeted at small/emerging farmers

May require BEE or transformation alignment

Best For

Infrastructure expansion

Land development

New greenhouse builds

Often co-funding required

Heavy reporting

Best For

Cold storage

Packaging plant

Export ambitions
