### 5.3 Commerce and procurement in scope

**Decision:** Yes, included in the platform scope.

**Implication:** Terra is not just an operations tool. It must cover:

- procurement
- incoming lots
- packhouse flow
- finished goods
- dispatch
- delivery
- customer and supplier records
- order-linked traceability

### 5.4 Traceability standard

**Decision:** Strict traceability from day one.

**Reason:** The standard must be high, with GMP-compliant farming practices as the target.

**Implication:**

- strict lot tracking
- stock movement logging
- SOP linkage
- staff attribution
- approval records where required
- harvest-to-customer traceability
- input-to-output linkage where practical

### 5.5 Production scope

**Decision:** Mixed farm from day one.

**Reason:** The user’s own operation and target customers usually include more than one production type.

**Implication:** Terra’s core architecture must support mixed-farm operations from the start, even if v0.1 only exposes hydroponics workflows first.

### 5.6 Worker navigation detail

**Decision:** Full location tree down to the lowest operational unit.

**Implication:** Tasks, logs, stock usage, and harvest records must all support deep location assignment such as:

- Field → Block → Section → Row
- Tunnel → Bay → DWC Tank → Raft
- Hall → System → Tank
- Room → Rack → Shelf

### 5.7 Task granularity

**Decision:** Tasks should remain separate and tied to a specific location unit.

**Reason:** Multiple teams may be working different jobs at once.

**Implication:**

- one task should not span multiple rows, rafts, tanks, or shelves by default
- task assignment and accountability stay precise
- reporting, labor tracking, and completion control remain cleaner

## 6) Direct recommendation

For Terra v1, the safest starting scope is:

- multi-site capable
- flexible location tree
- shared production system structure
- shared batch structure
- strong tasks and logs
- stock and asset linkage
- harvest linkage
- SOP linkage
- customer and sales linkage later unless needed immediately

That keeps the platform powerful without making version 1 too heavy.
