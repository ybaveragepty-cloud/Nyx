They allocate it like capital.

You need three buckets:

### 🔹 Operator Time (max 40%)

Farm oversight, problem solving, system checks.

If you’re physically involved in daily operations too much, you’re trapping yourself.

Your mission:  
Train or promote someone to handle 70–80% of day-to-day decisions.

You stay at:

- System level
- Financial level
- Expansion level

---

### 🔹 Architect Time (40%)

This is sacred.

This is where you:

- Document your blueprint
- Build holding structure
- Plan expansion
- Think strategically

No phone.  
No interruptions.  
Scheduled blocks.

Empire builders protect thinking time aggressively.

---

### 🔹 Relationship / Deal Time (20%)

- Investor conversations
- Strategic partners
- Legal advisors
- Key clients

If someone wants “just a quick chat,” it goes here or it doesn’t happen.
