# 🇿🇦 SOUTH AFRICA – BUSINESS FUNDING OPPORTUNITIES

---

# 1️⃣ GOVERNMENT FUNDING (Non-Repayable Grants & Incentives)

## 1.1 📍 Department of Agriculture (DALRRD / Provincial Agriculture)

**What it is:**  
Grants for primary production, infrastructure, irrigation, and emerging farmers.

**Pros**

- Non-repayable
- Infrastructure support
- Sometimes equipment subsidies
- Can unlock credibility for future funding

**Cons**

- Slow processing (6–18 months)
- Often politically influenced
- Paperwork heavy
- Usually targeted at small/emerging farmers
- May require BEE or transformation alignment

**Best For**

- Infrastructure expansion
- Land development
- New greenhouse builds

---

## 1.2 📍 dti / dtic (Department of Trade, Industry & Competition)

Programs:

- Agro-processing Support Scheme (APSS)
- Black Industrialists Scheme
- Manufacturing competitiveness incentives

**Pros**

- Large grants (R1m – R20m+ possible)
- Supports scaling & processing
- Good for packaging & value addition

**Cons**

- Requires strong compliance
- Often co-funding required
- Audited financials mandatory
- Heavy reporting

**Best For**

- Processing facility
- Cold storage
- Packaging plant
- Export ambitions

---

## 1.3 📍 SEFA (Small Enterprise Finance Agency)

**Type:** Government-backed loans (not grants)

**Pros**

- Lower interest than banks
- Agriculture-friendly
- Flexible security options

**Cons**

- Still debt
- Can be slow
- Risk assessment strict

**Best For**

- Working capital
- Equipment finance

---

## 1.4 📍 Land Bank

**Type:** Agricultural loans

**Pros**

- Designed for agriculture
- Understands farming cycles
- Long repayment terms possible

**Cons**

- Still debt
- Security required
- Sensitive to commodity risk

**Best For**

- Expansion
- Land purchase
- Major infrastructure

---

## 1.5 📍 IDC (Industrial Development Corporation)

**Type:** Large development funding

**Pros**

- Big funding (R5m+)
- Supports agro-industrial growth
- Can take equity or structured finance
- Long-term view

**Cons**

- Due diligence intense
- Must show job creation
- Not small-project friendly
- Slow

**Best For**

- 24-ton trout scale
- Multi-tunnel aquaponics facility
- Large RAS build
